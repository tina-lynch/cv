ABOUT

I once cruised the Caribbean and Baltic Seas as a photographer.




These days I’m working and playing in the world wide web of HTML, CSS & jQuery. I’m constantly learning new skills and right now I am building, designing and refining this site (from scratch!) and seeing where the balance of new and old skills meet.

With almost 3 years experience in front-end development I offer skills in translating design mock ups into responsive pages; building landing pages + HTML eDMS; and the ability to add interactive experiences to static designs. I have a strong work ethic and an eagerness to keep learning new skills amongst an ever-changing digital world.
