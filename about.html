EXPERIENCE/WORK HISTORY

  • AAT Kings Web Content Publisher | Nov 2015 - Present

    My current role as a web content publisher has evolved in the the skill set of a Front End Developer and is my biggest achievement and also the kick start to the (web) development of my career. With no prior coding skills, I was given a opportunity and the only way to succeed was to absorb and learn everything that got thrown my way. Today, I am part of the AAT Kings web team and my most recent project and highlight is my involvement in the re-design of the AAT Kings website. I have been heavily involved in bringing the new design to live via version control with HTML, CSS, jQuery and PHP. Currently in its final stages, aatkings.com will be a fully responsive, enhanced and modern website.

    Other projects I have undertaken vary from mobile optimisation, landing page creation (mock up to hard-code execution); and building functional and optimised eDMs with thorough testing across responsive platforms (desktop, mobile, tablet via resources like Litmus and BrowserStack). An achievement was further developing and implementing the eDMs into templates in Exact Target (SalesForce Marketing Cloud) for the wider Marketing Team to use, thus allowing the Web Team more time to focus on website development, functionality and enhancements.

    Further duties include maintaining and enhancing content/product load and working closely with internal stakeholders to ensure consistency and adherence to company guidelines. Extended duties and capabilities include video editing, SEO projects, knowledge of internal systems and documentation manuals for both consumer and internal stakeholders.

    Originally in Sales/Reservations, I moved into the Web Team through my self initiated request to shadow the Design Manager to gain exposure and experience in the Design and Marketing aspects of the business.
